About Me

I am London-dweller in her early 30s who aims to spread the word about my lifestyle tips, including local fitness classes, style and recipes.

What's my story?

I have a background in travel and customer service. I worked for many years as a flight attendant which, although was great fun, took it's toll on my emotional and physical wellbeing.

Lack of sleep coupled with not eating properly (sometimes not eating at all) and no time to exercise left me feeling incredibly down. This effected my relationships with those around me, and also the way I felt about myself.

I decided to leave this career in search of a new one, although I am still finding my feet! Whilst I am job searching, I like to blog about my Mum's amazing recipes, new exercise classes I have discovered in London, and great restaurants, amongst other things. I hope you enjoy my site!
